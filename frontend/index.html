How Book-Smart works

Set up in a morning. Run it all year.

No IT department needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Book-Smart.

1

Add your school

Sign up, add grades and classes. Import all learners from Excel in minutes — CEMIS numbers included.

2

Label your books

Print unique barcodes straight from the dashboard. Stick them on every textbook. No special hardware needed.

3

Scan to assign

Use any smartphone camera or a USB plug-and-play barcode scanner. Assign 40 books in under 8 minutes.

4

Recover losses

Run year-end audits automatically. Missing books trigger WhatsApp and SMS billing notices to parents.

Hardware scanner support

Works with any barcode scanner

Plug in any USB barcode scanner — the kind sold at Makro or game stores for under R200 — and Book-Smart instantly recognises it. No drivers, no setup, just plug and scan.

  • ✓ USB plug-and-play scanners (HID keyboard mode)
  • ✓ Bluetooth wireless scanners
  • ✓ Smartphone camera scanning (built-in)
  • ✓ Sound + visual feedback on every scan
  • ✓ Instant duplicate and conflict detection
  • ✓ Works offline — syncs when back online
